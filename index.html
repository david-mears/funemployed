Meta

How to play

One player is the employer. The rest are applicants.

The employer announces the role they are looking for (generated by clicking 'click here to post a new job ad'.

The applicants should then click 'click here to apply', which will reveal their skills and qualifications. These are the things the applicants will try to highlight in their interview.

Then, the players are called one by one to a short interview with the employer, during which the employer may ask any question relevant to the role, and the applicants should make the best case they can for their employment based on the qualities and experiences in their resume.

Depending on improv skills, you might find that it's most fun when the applicant doesn't have sight of their resume until the start of the interview. That way, applicants are not tempted to plan their interview when they should be listening to the other interviews. Alternatively, you could allow a short time for all the players to gather their thoughts in advance of the first interview.

Some interview question prompts are provided (with a 'get interview question prompt' button) if the interviewer would like inspiration.

After each round of interviews, the employer declares who they think did the best. They might find it helpful to take notes in the interview notes box, either to jog memory of each applicant or simply to get into the role better.

Finally, a new player takes over the role of employer for the next round.
